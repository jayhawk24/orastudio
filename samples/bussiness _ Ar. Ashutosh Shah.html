Highlighted Projects

Lockfield Home

The Lockfield Home is an off the grid home that our first to combat against dirty electricity. Solar panels, skylights and thermal concrete walls all work together to trap heat and power this home. Non-toxic materials were used as well.

Hooper Home

The Hooper Home is a restored farm house that features all green materials. Given a sleek modern interior, this home also features and underground tank used to store rain water to help reduce water costs. Solar panels were also added to the barn to assist with land maintenance.

State College Annex

We built a state of the art, fully LEED compliant science annex to our local state college. The walls are constructed from earth bricks that were made on sites and the greenhouse features Low-E windows.
